SOCIAL WORK

Kaar-e-kamal Welfare Foundation:

I am working with Kaar-e-Kamal Welfare Foundation.

Kaar-e-Kamal is a non-profit organization that dates back to October 6, 2018. It is purely a student-based organization. It was started by Mr. Bilal Yousaf along with his four friends, students of UET. It has gone so far by the grace of Allah Almighty that we have almost 1,700 Plus active members across the Punjab who work day and night for the betterment of society. The network of serving humanity through the platform of Kaar-e-Kamal has reached many campuses of other universities like BNU, PU, PIFD, LMDC, COMSATS, AMIC, UCP, UOS, and UOG, etc. Kaar-e-Kamal Welfare Society is serving in numerous cities of Pakistan by the grace of Allah Almighty including Multan, Gujranwala, Rahim yar Khan, Lahore, Bahawalpur, Haroonabad, Sahiwal,Islamabad, Faisalabad, Okara.
